What is Sephardi Central?

  • Halakha & Kabbalah
  • Nusachot & Chazzanut
  • Food & Culture


We hold daily discussions on sephardi halakha and kabbalah according to the daily parsha. We post the materials to read in our Zohar channel and then members can join in VC and discuss them with whoever wishes to join. We also have weekly shiurim in VC where you can ask questions live and discuss them in real time.


There are various styles of chazzanut and nusachot from the sephardi family. Moroccan, Iraqi, Yemeni, Spanish, etc. We link those mp3s on this site and discuss them in the server with anyone who wants to level up their chazzanut, piyutim or whatever.


Come share your favorite music, recipes, clothing, ceremonies and more with us. We speak Spanish, Ladino, Hebrew and Arabic and are always looking for new friends to join and learn from.
